# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2026-05-01**

X Silly Feed ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the X Silly Feed Chrome extension.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**X Silly Feed does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

- **No Data Collection**: We do not collect your name, email address, physical address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
- **No Browsing History**: We do not track or store your browsing history.
- **No Third-Party Analytics**: We do not use third-party analytics services to track your usage of the extension.

## 2. Local Processing

The extension operates entirely within your browser. All transformations to the X.com (Twitter) interface are performed locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

## 3. Storage

The extension uses Chrome's `storage` permission only to save your user preferences (e.g., toggle states). This data remains local to your browser and is not shared.

## 4. Compliance with Chrome Web Store Policies

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with the Google Chrome Web Store User Data Policy. We only request the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function:
- `storage`: To save user settings.
- `activeTab`: To apply styles to the current page.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
